BVRIT’s Film Making Club -
a place for aspiring filmmakers

Discover "Chalana Chithram BVRIT," a cinematic venture born from the minds of BVRIT's 2025 batch visionaries – Sreeram, Ganesh, Vidhya, Lavanya, and Surya Dhanush. With Dr. K. Dasaradha Ramaiah Garu's support, Head of IT and CSBS, we redefine storytelling through our film-making club. Join us in this creative journey!

17 April 2023

AGENDA

  • Unite passionate filmmakers, elevating skills and exploring the art of cinema.
  • Foster and promote filmmaking in all forms—short films, documentaries, music videos, and experiments.
  • Provide a supportive space for all interested students.
  • Organize workshops, screenings, and events for learning, creating, and showcasing your work.
"Chitrostav Film Festival: Launch Animation
Explore the essence of the Chitrostav Film Festival through our captivating launch animation crafted with After Effects.
BVRIT: Nature's Essence
Explore the serene surroundings of BVRIT College through beautifully captured cinematography, showcasing the tranquil environment that enriches the campus experience.
PANJA x OG: Fusion Edit
Immerse yourself in a unique cinematic experience as we blend the iconic style of "PANJA" with the mesmerizing soundtrack of "OG." Witness the seamless fusion of visuals and music, where the edgy editing of "PANJA" meets the soulful beats of "OG.
CANVAS: Short film
Follow the introspective journey of an introvert navigating the maze of social interaction. Will he embrace change or remain true to himself? The conclusion is left open, inviting viewers to paint their own endings onto this canvas of self-discovery.
Sankranthi: Village Essence
Immerse yourself in the cultural tapestry of Telangana's villages with our documentary capturing the essence of Sankranthi, the celebrated harvest festival. Through authentic footage captured on-location, delve into the heart of rural life as it unfolds amidst the rustic charm of village landscapes.

EVENTS

CHITROTSAV 2023 December

Chitrostav, organized by Chalana Chitram, was a fun-filled event for film lovers in our college. The two-day event featured exciting activities like Ad-making contests and film screenings. Students had a blast participating, making it a memorable experience.

48+48 HOURS Short Film Competition April 20223

Our first competition, the 48+48 Hour Short Film Contest, has significantly expanded our club's reputation beyond NITs to numerous regional engineering colleges. Participants were provided with a theme and tasked with creating a short film within the specified time frame.

Studio Inauguration December 2023

Vishnu Audio Visual studio, a center of excellence to provide a wider scope of teaching-learning activities like content creation, knowledge sharing was inaugurated in collaboration with Chalana Chitram on 2nd December, 2022

Film Making Workshop December 2022

Our inaugural event, a Film making workshop,aimed at film enthusiasts. We delved into the intricacies of filmmaking, covering key areas like cinematography, editing, screenwriting, and direction, establishing ourselves within the college community.This initiative solidified our identity as a team deeply passionate about the cinematic arts, propelling us closer to achieving our club's overarching mission.
